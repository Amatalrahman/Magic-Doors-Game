## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Run the script**

   ```
   python sec2.py
   ```

2. **Choose your option**
   - `0` - Rock 🪨
   - `1` - Paper 📄
   - `2` - Scissors ✂️

3. **See who wins**
   - Rock beats Scissors
   - Scissors beats Paper
   - Paper beats Rock
